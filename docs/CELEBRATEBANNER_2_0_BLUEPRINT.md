## 1. Executive Vision

### What CelebrateBanner 2.0 IS
- An **AI Celebration Studio** that takes a customer's raw, unsorted photos and returns finished, display-worthy celebration masterpieces.
- An **AI Creative Director** in software form: it curates, composes, and designs the way a professional would — the customer supplies memories and intent, not layout labor.
- A **reveal experience**: the emotional peak is the moment the customer sees four premium concepts generated from *their* photos, not a blank canvas they have to fill.
- A **celebration platform** spanning many life moments (graduation, championship, wedding, memorial, retirement, and more), unified by one standard of luxury quality.
- A **revenue engine** built on delighting customers into physical and digital products they are proud to display and gift.

### What CelebrateBanner 2.0 is NOT
- **Not a template picker.** Customers do not scroll a template gallery and pour photos into slots.
- **Not a DIY design tool.** We do not ask customers to be designers. The AI does the hard creative work.
- **Not a "use every photo" collage maker.** More photos is not the goal; the *best* result is.
- **Not a commodity banner printer.** Price and print size are outcomes, not the pitch.
- **Not a blank-canvas builder** where the burden of taste falls on the customer.

### Why the product must evolve beyond a banner builder
- A "banner builder" competes on features, price, and template count — a race to the bottom that is easy to copy.
- The current flow puts creative labor on the customer; the magic (and the moat) lives in the *AI doing the creative work*.
- The defensible position is **taste + curation + emotional payoff**, not tooling. An AI Celebration Studio is hard to copy because the value is in the judgment, not the buttons.
- Expanding beyond banners (into a full celebration ecosystem) multiplies lifetime value: one customer, many life moments, repeat celebrations.

## 2. Customer Experience

The new experience is designed around **one emotional arc**: intent → memories → anticipation → reveal → light personalization → ownership. The customer should feel guided, never asked to design.

### Step 1 — What are we celebrating today?
- Warm, human opening question — not a template grid.
- Customer selects the occasion (Graduation, Championship, Wedding, Memorial, etc.).
- This sets the AI Creative Director's emotional and stylistic context.
- Minimal friction: one clear choice, beautifully presented.

### Step 2 — Share your favorite memories
- Customer uploads photos freely — no slots, no counting anxiety.
- Framing is emotional ("Share your favorite memories"), not technical ("Upload up to N photos").
- The AI will decide which photos to use; the customer is reassured they don't need to curate.
- Smart handling of orientation, quality, and quantity happens invisibly.

### Step 3 — AI Creative Studio processing experience
- A **cinematic, intentional processing moment** — not a generic spinner.
- Communicates that real creative work is happening: selecting the best shots, choosing a hero, detecting the story, composing.
- Builds **anticipation** for the reveal. This wait is a feature, not a cost.
- Language reinforces craftsmanship: "Curating your best moments…", "Composing your masterpiece…".

### Step 4 — The Reveal — four premium concepts
- The emotional peak. The customer is presented **four distinct, finished concepts** built from their photos.
- Each concept is display-worthy on arrival — no concept looks like a rough draft.
- The four concepts: **Signature Edition, Luxury Gold, Family Legacy, Modern Editorial** (see §4).
- Presentation is cinematic — concepts reveal with intention, inviting "I can't believe it created this."

### Step 5 — Personalize only if needed
- Personalization is **optional and light**, never required.
- The default output is already great; edits are refinements (swap a word, adjust a name, nudge a color), not construction.
- The customer is never handed a blank canvas or forced into a design tool.
- Guardrails preserve the luxury quality bar even during edits.

### Step 6 — Bring your masterpiece home
- Clear, confident path to ownership: digital download, print, framed, gallery, or a full Celebration Collection.
- Upsells are framed as *more ways to enjoy and share* the masterpiece, not as friction.
- Trust signals (quality guarantee, print specs, delivery clarity) support the purchase.

## 3. AI Creative Director

The AI is not a filter or a layout randomizer. It thinks like a seasoned art director. Its mandate:

- **Select best photos** — evaluate sharpness, exposure, faces, composition, and emotional strength; use the strongest, not all.
- **Choose hero image** — identify the single most powerful photo to anchor the design.
- **Detect story** — infer the narrative (a graduate's journey, a championship run, a life remembered) and let it drive layout.
- **Improve composition** — crop, balance, and place photos for professional visual flow.
- **Avoid overcrowding** — restraint over density; whitespace is a design element.
- **Create emotional hierarchy** — hero dominates; supporting photos support; text and accents guide the eye.
- **Prefer beauty over using every photo** — a stunning design with 5 photos beats a cluttered one with 30. **Leaving photos out is a valid, encouraged creative decision.**

**Governing principle:** When in doubt, the AI chooses the more beautiful, more restrained, more display-worthy result — even if that means using fewer photos and fewer words.

## 4. Four Premium Concepts

Every reveal presents these four concepts, each a complete and distinct design point of view.

### Signature Edition
- **Emotion:** Timeless pride, confident elegance.
- **Visual style:** Refined, balanced, editorial-classic; the "safe but stunning" default that flatters any occasion.
- **Ideal use case:** The customer who wants something beautiful and universally impressive — graduations, achievements, milestones.
- **Layout principles:** Strong central hero, disciplined grid for supporting photos, generous margins, clear focal path.
- **Typography:** Cormorant Garamond display headings paired with Outfit for supporting text; high contrast, elegant hierarchy.
- **Photo treatment:** Clean, true-to-life color; subtle unifying grade; crisp edges.
- **Decorative effects:** Thin gold accent lines, restrained framing, soft depth.

### Luxury Gold
- **Emotion:** Opulence, celebration, "this is a big deal."
- **Visual style:** Rich, glamorous, high-contrast; deep obsidian grounds with champagne-gold radiance.
- **Ideal use case:** Championships, galas, major honors, anniversaries — moments that deserve a red-carpet feel.
- **Layout principles:** Dramatic hero with spotlight emphasis, luxurious negative space, gold framing devices, symmetrical grandeur.
- **Typography:** Large Cormorant Garamond display in gold gradients; letter-spaced accents; premium small caps for labels.
- **Photo treatment:** Cinematic grade, deepened shadows, warm highlights, gentle vignette to focus the hero.
- **Decorative effects:** Champagne-gold gradients, cinematic lighting/glow, foil-like accents, elegant corner flourishes.

### Family Legacy
- **Emotion:** Warmth, belonging, generational love.
- **Visual style:** Heartfelt and storytelling-driven; heirloom quality that feels made to be passed down.
- **Ideal use case:** Family reunions, memorials, retirements, anniversaries — many-people, many-memory occasions.
- **Layout principles:** Narrative flow (timeline or gathered-memories arrangement), hero plus meaningful supporting cluster, breathing room around faces.
- **Typography:** Classic, warm serif display with humanist body text; approachable elegance over austerity.
- **Photo treatment:** Soft, warm tone; gentle unifying grade that harmonizes photos from different eras/cameras.
- **Decorative effects:** Subtle textured background, soft edges, understated gold detailing, tasteful captions/dates when they add meaning.

### Modern Editorial
- **Emotion:** Bold, current, confidently stylish.
- **Visual style:** Magazine-grade, asymmetric, high-fashion; strong grids and dramatic scale contrast.
- **Ideal use case:** Social graphics, senior night, sports, brand-forward and younger audiences.
- **Layout principles:** Editorial asymmetry, oversized hero, deliberate off-grid tension, bold negative space, strong typographic blocks.
- **Typography:** Large, confident type; tight tracking; sans-forward hierarchy with a refined serif accent.
- **Photo treatment:** Punchy contrast, modern color grade, decisive crops.
- **Decorative effects:** Geometric accents, bold rules, restrained gold as a highlight rather than a theme.

## 5. Design Standards — The CelebrateBanner Quality Bar

Non-negotiable standards. Every generated design and every product surface must meet these:

- **Luxury by default** — the baseline output looks premium, never "budget."
- **Strong hero image** — every design is anchored by one dominant, well-chosen photo.
- **Clean spacing** — generous, intentional whitespace; nothing cramped.
- **Premium typography** — Cormorant Garamond + Outfit; disciplined hierarchy; no default/system-font look.
- **Gold accents** — champagne-gold used with restraint as the signature of the brand.
- **Cinematic lighting** — depth, glow, and grading that make photos feel elevated.
- **No clutter** — restraint over density; the eye always knows where to go.
- **No cheap templates** — nothing that reads as a generic, mass-market template.
- **No random photo placement** — every photo position is a deliberate compositional decision.
- **Every design must be worthy of display** — if it wouldn't look good framed on a wall or shared with pride, it fails the bar.

## 6. Revenue Model

A layered model designed to delight first, monetize through pride and gifting, and grow lifetime value.

- **Free AI preview** — the reveal of four concepts is free. The WOW comes before the wallet. This is the top of the funnel and the core acquisition engine.
- **Digital download** — instant, high-resolution digital file(s). Flat, accessible price point.
- **Standard print** — professionally printed banner/poster at spec (300 DPI, CMYK).
- **Premium print** — elevated arrangement / premium materials and finish.
- **Framed product** — ready-to-display framed masterpiece; a giftable, high-perceived-value tier.
- **Gallery edition** — top-tier physical product (premium materials, sizing, presentation) for the customer who wants the definitive version.
- **Celebration Collection** — a bundle of matching assets around one celebration (see Sprint 2): social graphics, wallpapers, thank-you card, extra prints.
- **Premium AI upgrades** — enhanced AI capabilities (e.g., additional concepts, advanced retouching, extra variations, priority processing) as paid enhancements.
- **Partnerships** — schools, teams, event organizers, print/frame providers, and gifting channels.
- **Future membership** — recurring plan for families/organizations that celebrate often (see §8 Sprint 3): saved library, reorders, reminders, member pricing.

> **Pricing note:** Existing pricing is governed by the current app and Printmoz reseller confirmation. This blueprint defines the *model shape*; it does not authorize any pricing change. Do not change pricing without Printmoz confirmation.
